== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WooCommerce 3.0 or higher

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Hide Shipping Method For WooCommerce, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type Hide Shipping Method For WooCommerce and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our plugin you can view details about it such as the the rating and description. Most importantly, of course, you can install it by simply clicking Install Now.

= Manual Installation =

1. Unzip the files and upload the folder into your plugins folder (/wp-content/plugins/) overwriting older versions if they exist
2. Activate the plugin in your WordPress admin area.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What WordPress versions are compatible with this plugin? =

The Hide Shipping Method for WooCommerce Plugin is compatible with WordPress versions from 3.8 up to 5.3, ensuring broad compatibility across various site architectures.

= Which versions of WooCommerce does this plugin support? =

This plugin is fine-tuned for WooCommerce versions 2.6 and above, guaranteeing seamless integration with a wide range of WooCommerce releases.

=Can I hide only specific shipping methods when free shipping is available? =

Absolutely! Our plugin allows you to hide particular shipping methods whenever free shipping becomes available. Simply navigate to the plugin settings, select the relevant configuration option, and specify the shipping methods you wish to conceal. Your settings are activated immediately after saving, applying your preferences to the storefront.

= Is it possible to hide all shipping methods when free shipping or local pickup is selected? =

Yes, with ease. The plugin provides an option to hide all shipping methods when either free shipping or local pickup is chosen for the order, streamlining your shipping options efficiently.

= How do I hide a custom shipping method with this plugin? =

To hide a custom shipping method, visit the product page where this shipping method is applicable. Locate the slug (found in the 'value' field) for the custom shipping method and incorporate it into your list of shipping methods to be hidden during checkout.

= Can the plugin hide shipping methods based on specific conditions beyond free shipping? =

The plugin excels in versatility, offering features to hide shipping methods under various conditions, such as:
Based on Shipping Classes: Conceal shipping options based on the classes defined in your WooCommerce settings, including selecting specific classes or products without any shipping class.
Conditional Hide Shipping: Develop basic or advanced rules for hiding shipping methods based on delivery dates, times, products, categories, and more.

= Can shipping methods be hidden based on the destination country or state? =

Yes, the premium version of the plugin allows you to hide shipping methods depending on the destination country or state, providing more control over your shipping options.

= Is it possible to manage multiple hide shipping options? =

Absolutely. The plugin facilitates the management of numerous hide shipping rules, letting you specify different conditions for when to display or hide shipping methods to cater to varied logistics strategies.

= Can the plugin hide shipping methods based on the total order weight? =

Yes, one of the practical use cases of our plugin is the ability to hide shipping methods based on the total weight of the order, enhancing the customization of shipping options for your store.

= Does the plugin support hiding shipping for specific user roles? =

Indeed, the plugin provides a sophisticated feature to hide shipping methods based on user roles, allowing for tailored shipping experiences for different customer segments.

= Can I receive customer support if I encounter issues with the plugin? =

Certainly! We pride ourselves on top-of-the-line customer support. Our team is responsive, fast, and ready to offer clear and quick solutions to any issues you may face, ensuring an exceptional experience with our plugin.
